Visions and Mission

As per the vision of our Prime Minister Sh. Narendra Modi and the initiatives by MHRD for development of villages, Unnat Bharat Scheme is launched and a mandate to develop 5 villages is given to MNIT Jaipur.

Unnat Bharat Abhiyan is inspired by the vision of transformational change in rural development processes by leveraging knowledge institutions to help build the architecture of an Inclusive India..

Unnat Bharat Abhiyan is conceptualised as a movement to enable processes that connect institutes of higher education with local communities to address the development challenges of rural India.

It also aims to create a virtuous cycle between the society and an inclusive university system by providing knowledge and practices for emerging professions and to upgrade the capabilities of both the public and the private sectors
