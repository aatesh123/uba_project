Dhab Ka Nala Village

Dhab Ka Nala Village

Report(16 Feb 2019)

Introduction :


Unnat Bharat Abhiyan (UBA), MNIT Jaipur has organized a trip to Dhab ka Nala village on 16 Feb. 2019. The objective of this trip was to inculcate the science and technology in the school students of the village through models , charts, and videos. The team of 12 members has visited the village. The team reached the school at 9:30 a.m. and participated in the school prayer. The students also asked various doubts from various subjects like Mathematics, science & environment to the team and got the answers. The team got warm and positive feedback from the school principal.

The team conducted following activities during the trip:
1. Volcano Model
2. Magnet experiment
3. Drawing competition for 1st and 2nd class students
4. Balloon fight for 1st and 2nd class students
5. Learning words through Crossword
