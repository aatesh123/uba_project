Bhurthal Village Report(22 Sep 2018)

MNIT Jaipur had organized a medical camp in Bhurthal Village on 15th April 2017. At medical camp basic medical treatment was provided and general checkup was held to help people be aware of their health status. MNIT Jaipur had organized a team of doctors, nurses and a lab technician who worked tirelessly to see that every patient is attended to. The biggest percentage of cases seen involve diarrhea and back pains. The Medical Camp was coordinated by Dr. M. L. Meena, Dr. Sushant Upadhyaya.

MNIT UBA Team taught Aanganbadi students also and played Football with students and Organized running competition.They taught students about internet use
